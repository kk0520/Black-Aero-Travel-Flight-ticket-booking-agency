Welcome to Black Air International

Black Air International is an airline tickets reservation company. The company has numerous branches all over the world. In the past, BAI used to handle all its flight operations manually, but since the population of flyers has increased over the years, it had become a tedious job to do this manually. So, the company shifted its management to a small scale Java based application, to help customers get a flight ticket booked or cancelled in coordination with their travel agency.
